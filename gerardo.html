About

Professional video game 3D Artist and Animator based in Los Angeles. Making games since 2017, I have worked in many aspects of development including 3D modeling, animation, and programming.

I have a Computer Animation degree, Animation Production degree, Computer Science degree, and Video Game Animation certificate from College of the Canyons where I started the Animation Club in Fall 2018 and served as President, joined the Computer Science Club in Spring 2017, and served as Vice President & Treasurer of the club from Fall 2017 to Spring 2019 where I worked on a plethora of games.

While I’m not making groovy video games, I’m mostly likely playing video games or building lego sculptures.
