Take a Breath
(Mini Jam 73 "Power" Game Jam)
Images:

Your spaceship is low on power and you're trapped in an asteroid belt! You can only divert power to one system at a time! Activate your thrusters to move, bash asteroids with your shield, or refill your oxygen. Navigate your way to each of the power cores then make your way out of the asteroid belt. Just don't forget to Take a Breath.

Made for the Mini "Power" game jam, it was made as a navigational resource management game. I did all of the art assets with the jam's limitation of "only use 5 colors" to make it look like a retro GB game. I fully solo developed the game creating all of the environment art, player art, animation, programming, level design, and systems design myself in just 3 days.

Link to download: https://screams-from-the-cellar.itch.io/take-a-breath

Rewound Running Remix
(Brackeys 2020.2 "Rewind" Game Jam )
Images:

This game is a platforming auto-runner... in reverse!? The player must press the 'jump' button as the player would normally be jumping as the whole level rewinds to the very beginning in this rhythm game disguised as a 2D platformer.

Made for the Brackeys "Rewind" game jam. I did all of the environment art and background art for the game as well as the UI/UX art and general game design in just 4 days.

Link to download: https://persomatey.itch.io/rewound-running-remix
Link to GitHib repository: https://github.com/Persomatey/ProjectRewindRhythm

Legend of Fantasy
(COC CSC Spring 2019)
Images:

A top-down Zelda-like game with a fantasy theme. Fight your way through droves of villainous monsters such as orcs and mushrooms to make your way to the end to defeat the scourge of the Fantaswhere Forest, Orcutoryx.

As my last year as an Officer of the Computer Science Club, I integrated all of the animations for the game into the Unity game engine and made many of the UI assets for the game. I also helped with the overall systems design and level design of the project as well as programmed the Mushroom Enemy AI.

Link to download: https://coc-computerscienceclub.itch.io/legend-of-fantasy
Link to GitHib repository: https://github.com/CanyonsCSClub/Spring2019

Space Shooter 9000
(COC CSC Fall 2017)
Images:

A side-scrolling space shooter arcade game with a low poly aesthetic. Battle through wave after wave of enemy ships as you plunge deep into enemy territory.

My first game with the Computer Science Club, we went for a simple arcade shooter. I operated as lead artist and lead animator for this project. I 3D modeled and animated the player's default space ship and some of the enemy space ships. I did the concept art for all of the space ships in the game.

Link to download: https://coc-computerscienceclub.itch.io/space-shooter-9000
Link to GitHib repository: https://github.com/CanyonsCSClub/Fall2017
